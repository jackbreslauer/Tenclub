# Tenclub Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview

Tenclub is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Screen Time Data

Tenclub uses Apple's Screen Time API to count your daily phone pickups. This data:

- Is processed entirely on your device
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never shared with third parties
- Cannot be accessed by the developer

Apple controls access to Screen Time data through the Family Controls framework. You can revoke this access at any time in your device's Settings.

## Data We Collect

**None.** Tenclub does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry.

## Data Storage

All pickup count data remains on your device and is managed by Apple's Screen Time system. Tenclub does not maintain any external databases or servers.

## Third-Party Services

Tenclub does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Tenclub does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version within the app and update the "Last Updated" date above.
